Honeycomb Toffee From Squid Game

Description

A recent TV-Show from Korea on Netflix has been huge hit worldwide! Specially the episode 3 where participants play a particular game where they have to carve out shape from a circular Honeycomb Toffee.

The participants have to carve the shape without breaking the circle, else they get shot to death. Scary right? Nonetheless it is important to note that the Toffee looks very appealing and easy to make!

    Ingredients (Only 2 household Ingredients!)
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
    Steps (source)
  1. Carmalize the sugar.
  2. Add the baking soda and mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture into silicone mat.
  4. Use press to make sure it has even flat surface.
  5. Use cookie cutter to add shape in the centre. Leave it to cool and enjoy.
